# Spec / People And Commitment Schemas

People files and commitment files are the primary compiled profile surfaces.

People files should emphasize:

- who the person is
- relationship context
- contact rhythm
- open obligations
- recent materially relevant evidence

Commitment files should emphasize:

- current status
- priority
- deadline or timeframe
- stakeholders
- open loops
- dated evidence or progress trail

Use wikilinks to connect people, commitments, related claims, and important events.
